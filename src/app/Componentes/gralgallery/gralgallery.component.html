"Goo Wolf", was an illustration created in a mirror image, the person in that painting is someone I know and who always said "I am someone cold and without feelings".

But he is wrong, he seeks strength in not show vulnerability. I hope that when the years pass, he looks at the painting again and realizes what I saw in it.

The main character of this painting is a Korean actor that I met years ago playing a romantic series: "Never stop smiling, you light up the world of everyone who is in front of you."

They are two people I met in my past, my path and my ideals made us take different paths, but it's okay, it's good to meet different people and it's also good to recognize that one should let those people go from their life.

I'm not good at saying goodbye and I never will be, but at least I can keep you in one of my paintings forever.
